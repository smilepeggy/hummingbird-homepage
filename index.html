About

Hummingbird

The acoustic group of acoustic guitar, vocals and percussion is from Tainan National University Cheng Kung Guitar Club. Since we have spent some time on the East Coast of Taiwan, we hope to bring the warmth of Tainan and the comfort of the East Coast to the music.


The style of music from folk songs of the 1950~1960s to pop songs of today, and the performance style is free and spontaneous without losing its essence.

Members

Who we are

Peggy Wang

出生於台灣高雄市。 熱愛分享，喜歡與人討論，探索各種音樂、知識。 擔任樂團主唱，擅長口哨以及卡祖笛。 喜歡隨意哼唱歌曲，並把故事寫成音樂。


Born in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. I love to share, discuss with others, and explore various music and knowledge. As the lead singer of the band, I specialize in whistling and kazoo. Enjoys humming random songs and writing stories to music.

Chen Chun Shuo

出生於台灣台中市。 熱愛嘗試各種曲風。 擔任Hummingbird樂團樂手，擅長把主唱哼唱的音符編織成美好的旋律。 嘗試各種曲風，現在於中國餐廳擔任樂團鋼琴手，持續進步中。


Born in Taichung, Taiwan. I have a passion for experimenting with various music styles. As the instrumental performer of Hummingbird, I am good at weaving the notes hummed by the Peggy into beautiful melodies. I am now working as a pianist in a Chinese restaurant and continue to improve.
