About

Hummingbird

The acoustic group of acoustic guitar, vocals and percussion is from Tainan National University Cheng Kung Guitar Club. Since we have spent some time on the East Coast of Taiwan, we hope to bring the warmth of Tainan and the comfort of the East Coast to the music.


The style of music from folk songs of the 1950~1960s to pop songs of today, and the performance style is free and spontaneous without losing its essence.
